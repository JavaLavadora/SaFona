The Story

Balchar was napping against a talayot when his myotragus companion Bep wandered off and ate a dimoni's sacred herbs. Now Bep can talk (and won't shut up), every dimoni on the island wants a piece of him, and Balchar is dragged along for the ride through Mallorca's history — from the talayotic era to Roman occupation, the age of pirates, and beyond.

He just wanted a nap.

Features

  • Sling-based combat — tap for quick shots, hold to charge devastating attacks
  • Wall jumping & platforming — precise movement across talayotic ruins, Roman aqueducts, and pirate ships
  • Boss encounters — each world ends with a unique boss fight with distinct mechanics
  • Dimoni mask powers — earn supernatural abilities like Stone Slam, Double Jump, and Fire Dash
  • Bep, your companion — a cursed myotragus who provides hints, commentary, and accidental time travel
  • Shop system — spend stones at Llorenç's shop for upgrades and consumables
  • Multiple worlds — each inspired by a real era of Balearic history and folklore
  • 16-bit pixel art — SNES-era aesthetic with parallax scrolling backgrounds

Controls

  • Move — Arrow keys / A, D
  • Jump — Space
  • Attack — X
  • Interact — Enter
  • Mask Power — Z
  • Pause — Escape

About

Sa Fona blends real Mallorcan history and folklore with lighthearted parody — serious cultural themes seen through the eyes of a protagonist who wants absolutely nothing to do with any of it. Inspired by Shrek's reluctant hero tone, Crash Bandicoot's mask power-ups, and Shovel Knight's retro platformer structure.

Built with Pygame. Open source under GPL-3.0:
https://github.com/JavaLavadora/SaFona
